    About Mukun

    Teacher & Facilitator
    of Sacred Arts

    Mukun Verma is a devoted Sound Healing Teacher, Conscious Breathwork Facilitator, and Energy Work Practitioner — guiding souls toward deep inner alignment through the ancient wisdom of the Himalayas.

    Himalayan Healing School is a space that teaches how to use sound for therapy and spiritual purposes — through Tibetan Singing Bowls, Crystal Bowls, Tuning Forks, Gongs, Crystal Healing and Mantra Chanting.

    With a vibrant and nurturing energy, Mukun weaves together sacred sound, conscious breath, and healing energy to create transformative experiences that heal the body, calm the mind, and awaken the spirit.
