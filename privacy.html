Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Himalayan Healing School ("we", "Mukun Verma", "the School") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit our website or contact us, and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information you voluntarily provide to us. This may include:

  • Your name and email address when you send a message through our contact form
  • Your WhatsApp number if you initiate a conversation via our WhatsApp links
  • Any information you share in messages (e.g., questions about sessions or courses)

We do not use cookies for tracking, run advertising, or collect browsing data. Our website is a static site hosted on GitHub Pages — no analytics scripts are loaded.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information you share is used solely to:

  • Respond to your enquiry about sessions, courses, or retreats
  • Send you information about upcoming sessions if you have asked to be notified
  • Process any booking or course registration

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

3. Language Preference

Our website remembers your language preference (e.g., English, Czech, French, Spanish) using your browser's local storage. This data stays on your device only and is never sent to any server.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website links to and loads content from the following third parties:

  • Google Fonts — for typography (fonts are loaded from Google's servers)
  • WhatsApp — clicking WhatsApp links opens the WhatsApp app or website
  • Instagram — links to our Instagram profiles
  • Google Maps — an embedded map shows our location in Rishikesh
  • Formspree — if you submit our contact form, your message is processed by Formspree (see Formspree's Privacy Policy)

Each of these services has its own privacy policy which governs how they handle data.

5. Data Retention

Contact messages are kept only as long as needed to serve your enquiry. You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing us.

6. Your Rights (EU / GDPR)

If you are based in the European Union, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, please contact us directly.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects when changes were last made.
